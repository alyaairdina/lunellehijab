Care Guide

Gentle Washing

Hand wash using cold water and mild detergent to protect the fabric and preserve its colour.

Air Drying

Air dry naturally in a shaded area and avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Low Heat

Iron using a low temperature setting to maintain the fabric's texture and quality.

Proper Storage

Fold neatly or hang securely in a cool, dry place to prevent wrinkles and damage.
