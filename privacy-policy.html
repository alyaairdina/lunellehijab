Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lunelle Hijab collects, uses and safeguards information when you visit our website or place an order. This website was developed solely for educational purposes for the IML254 Introduction to Web Content Development course and is not the official website of Lunelle Hijab.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, create an account or contact us, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, delivery address and payment preference.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders

  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To improve our website, products and services

Cookies

Our website may use browser storage to remember items in your shopping cart and your preferences, such as whether you've already seen a promotional pop-up. This information stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, as this is an educational project, please avoid entering real sensitive personal or payment information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites, such as social media pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
