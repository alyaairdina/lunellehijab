Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Find quick answers about orders, shipping, payment and returns below.

Orders & Shipping

How long does delivery take?

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 working days and delivered within 3-5 working days across Malaysia.

How much does shipping cost?

We charge a flat shipping rate of RM 8.00 for every order, regardless of order size.

Can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, a tracking number will be sent to the email address you provided at checkout.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Online Banking, E-Wallet and Cash On Delivery at checkout.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! Join as a member for 10% off your first order, or use the code BUY2SAVE10 when you buy any two hijabs in one order.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if unworn, unwashed and in original packaging. Visit our Return Policy page for full details.

How do I request a return or exchange?

Simply email us at lunellehijabs@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return, and our team will guide you through the process.

Products & Sizing

Are your hijabs one size?

Most of our shawls and squares are one-size and designed with flexible draping to suit most head sizes.

What materials do you use?

Our collections use fabrics such as Premium Chiffon, Cotton Voile and Korean Moss Crepe. Each product page lists its exact material.

Account

Do I need an account to order?

No, you can check out as a guest. Creating an account simply makes future checkouts faster.

How do I contact customer support?

Reach us anytime at lunellehijabs@gmail.com or +60 12-345 6789, or chat with Luna, our virtual assistant.
