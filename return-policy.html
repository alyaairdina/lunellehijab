Return Policy

Not the right fit or shade? Here's how returns and exchanges work at Lunelle Hijab.

Last Updated: July 2026

Return Eligibility

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of receiving your order, provided the item is unworn, unwashed, unaltered and returned in its original packaging with tags attached.

How To Start A Return

  • Email lunellehijabs@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return
  • Our team will confirm your eligibility and share the return address
  • Pack the item securely and ship it back within 3 days of approval
  • Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund or exchange

Refunds

Approved refunds will be credited back to your original payment method within 5-7 working days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Exchanges

Need a different colour or style? We're happy to exchange your item, subject to availability. If the replacement costs more, we'll ask you to pay the difference; if it costs less, we'll refund the balance.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked as Final Sale
  • Accessories such as brooches and pins, for hygiene reasons
  • Items showing signs of wear, washing or alteration

Damaged Or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item so we can arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

Contact For Returns

For any return or exchange enquiries, reach out to us at lunellehijabs@gmail.com or +60 12-345 6789.
