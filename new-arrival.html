Care Guide

Washing

Hand wash in cold water with a mild detergent, or use the delicate cycle. Avoid bleach to keep colours vibrant.

Drying

Air dry in shade away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and preserve fabric elasticity.

Ironing

Use a low heat setting when ironing to maintain the fabric's quality and smooth appearance.

Storage

Fold neatly or hang to avoid deep creases. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat.
