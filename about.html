About Lunelle Hijab

A modern Malaysian hijab brand crafted for women who move through busy days without giving up on elegance.

OUR STORY

Grace In Every Fold

Lunelle Hijab began with a simple frustration: hijabs that looked beautiful in photos but felt stiff, warm or slipped out of place by midday. We wanted something different, a hijab that could keep up with back-to-back meetings, early mornings, long commutes and everything in between, without asking you to choose between comfort and elegance.

The name "Lunelle" draws from the quiet, steady glow of moonlight, and "Grace In Every Fold" is our reminder that elegance isn't about perfection. It's in the small, everyday moments: the fold of a shawl before a morning meeting, the ease of an instant hijab on a rushed day, the confidence of stepping out feeling like yourself.

OUR MISSION

What Drives Us

To provide elegant, comfortable and affordable hijabs for modern Muslim women, so that quality and modest style are never out of reach.

Quality

Premium fabrics and careful finishing, checked piece by piece before it reaches you.

Comfort

Lightweight, breathable materials designed for real, all-day wear, not just photos.

Affordability

Elegant style shouldn't come with a luxury price tag. We keep quality accessible.

Modesty

Every design is thoughtfully created to honour modest fashion without compromise.
